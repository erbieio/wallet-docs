Account#

Create an Account#

  1. Click the Create an Account button.

  1. Enter your password and confirm, and click Create an Account.

Import your Account#

  1. Click Import your Account.

  1. Paste your private key, click Import, and then you will get your account back.

Copy Wallet Address#

Click on the Wallet Address on the main page.

Add a New Account#

Click your account name, and click Create a New Account.
